# Thinking Ledger

## Problem

What is the exact problem, bug, or design question?

## Current Evidence

What code, logs, docs, tests, or user reports did you inspect?

## Assumptions

What must be true for your plan to work?

## Alternatives

What options did you consider, and why did you reject them?

## Chosen Approach

What are you changing, and why is this the smallest responsible move?

## Verification

What will prove the change works?

## Residual Risk

What could still be wrong after this passes?
